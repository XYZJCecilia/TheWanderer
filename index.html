The Story of the Wanderer

This is the story of a Genshin Impact Character who is now being called the Wanderer.

The journey of the being "Wanderer" in the Genshin history since his first appearance

  1. Before the Wanderer's official release as a playable character
    • 1.1: Appearance in the Event Quest leaving the quote "The sky is fake."
    • 2.1: Inazuma Archon Quest running the Delusion factory
    • 2.4: Inazuma Event Quest tempered with the blade diagram
    • 3.1: Sumeru Archon Quest being the new God in making
    • 3.2: Sumeru Archon Quest being defeated by the traveler

  2. After 3.2: Drip marketing
    • 3.3: officially become playable and appeared in the Archon quest interlude chapter.

Artifact Description of Wanderer's background story

He was born with a face fairer than any other,
Destined to a long life and a hollow will.


He was a transcendent being, divinely created, but he was cast aside like worthless dross.
Yet, due to an error that cannot be known, he roused himself from slumber,
And began to wander the mortal realm.


Before the Fatui found him, he had drifted for countless years
And in that time, this is what his experience had taught him:


"I am a 'human' who surpasses all others."
"Even the gods daren't meddle in my fate."
"Neither mortal nor god, nor fate itself, is qualified to be my judge."
"I am free to choose how I wish to spend the remainder of my days."


"Since these mask-wearing people are so fun to be around..."
"I think I'll become one of them."
